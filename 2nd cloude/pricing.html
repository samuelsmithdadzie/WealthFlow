Common Questions

Is WealthFlow really free? +

Yes — the Free plan is genuinely free forever. You get the full dashboard, expense logger, budget planner, survival predictor, gamification, and the opportunity hub. No credit card needed, ever.

Is my financial data safe? +

Your data is stored securely in your browser's local storage and optionally in the cloud. We never sell personal data to third parties, and you can delete your account and all data at any time.

Can I cancel Premium anytime? +

Yes. Cancel any time with no questions asked. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your billing period, then automatically return to the Free plan.

Does WealthFlow work outside Ghana? +

Yes! All the financial tools work for any African student. The platform is optimized for Ghanaian students (GHS currency, local scholarships), but we're expanding to more countries soon.

What is the AI Financial Coach? +

It's a conversational AI powered by Claude (Anthropic) that knows your real financial data — your balance, spending patterns, streak, and goals. You can ask it anything: "Can I afford a new phone?" or "How do I save faster?" and it gives personalized, math-backed answers.
